About StrokePredict

StrokePredict is a machine learning-based web application designed to help individuals assess their risk of stroke based on key health indicators such as age, glucose level, blood pressure, and medical history.

Our goal is to provide early warning signs using AI to support proactive health care decisions. This tool is not a substitute for professional medical advice but a supplementary screening aid.

How It Works

  • Enter basic medical information such as age, gender, hypertension, glucose level, and more.
  • The model processes the inputs using a trained machine learning algorithm.
  • The system displays the likelihood (probability) of stroke risk.
  • You can use the result to make informed decisions or consult a healthcare provider.
